Privacy Policy & Data Deletion

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account using Email, Google, or Facebook, we collect your basic profile information (such as your Name and Email Address) to facilitate order creation and history tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to process your grocery requisitions, generate invoices, and communicate with you regarding your orders (e.g., fulfillment dates and pickup/delivery logistics).

3. User Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in via Facebook, Google, or Email, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your data from our systems.

How to delete your data:

  1. Send an email to privacy@your-domain.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  3. We will purge all your personal records, order history, and authentication data within 7 business days and confirm via email.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with unauthorized third parties. We use secure cloud platforms (such as Supabase and Cloudflare) solely for database management and secure authentication routing.
